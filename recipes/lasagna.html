Tanya's Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

This recipe has been passed down through Tanya's family for decades, here's how she's making it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (15 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (15 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese and spaghetti sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of spaghetti sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  7. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) oven for 30-35 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
